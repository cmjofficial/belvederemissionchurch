About Us

We are a Christ-centered community committed to worship, discipleship, and service. Our mission is to share the Gospel and help people grow in faith.

Our Ministries

A ministry for young people to grow in faith, build friendships, and serve God together. Activities include youth fellowships, Bible studies, worship nights, and outreach programs.

The Children’s Ministry nurtures young hearts in faith, teaching them God’s Word and helping them grow in Christ at an early age.

The Worship Team is dedicated to glorifying God by leading the congregation in Christ-centered praise and worship. Through music, singing, and instrumental excellence, the team seeks to create an atmosphere where people can experience God’s presence, prepare their hearts to receive His Word, and grow in deeper relationship with Him
